Lasagna

The Perfect Lasagna!!!

Here is how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef (or turkey)
  • 2 large eggs
  • Marinara sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook ground beef and onions until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add marinara sauce and simmer.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Italian seasoning.
